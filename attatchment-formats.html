Attatchment Formats

When creating a new post, you can attatch one image, video or audio file. Before uploading, make sure the attatchment format is compatible with Aurora and other clients of STiBaRC.

Images


It is recommended that image files are "PNG" or a "JPG (JPEG)".

Video


It is recommended that video files are "mp4". If you are uploading a video from an iOS device, please note that others WILL NOT be able to view your video because the "mov" format is not supported on other devices.

Audio


It is recommended that audio files are "mp3". You can use an online audio converter on your PC or compatible Android OS device to convert audio files to "mp3" by clicking / tapping the "Convert Audio" button below. Please note this site is not part of Aurora and is governed by seperate policies.
