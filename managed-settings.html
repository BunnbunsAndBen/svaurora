MANAGED SETTINGS




"domain Some settings are managed by your administrator" is displayed when an administrator manages your personal settings on the STiBaRC services. Your administrator can enable, disable and / or change your settings on both Aurora and STiBaRC. Contact your administrator regarding managed settings.
"domain Some settings are managed" is displayed when you are not logged into Aurora.

The following settings are managed when you are not logged into Aurora:
  • My Account
  • Security & Privacy
  • Notifcations & Alerts
  • Messenger
Additionally, the following features are disabled on Aurora:
  • Creating Posts
  • Commenting on Posts
  • The post rating (Up-votes & Down-votes) feature
  • Aurora Messenger
  • Following another user
To solve this, simply log in to Aurora using the "Login with STiBaRC method".
