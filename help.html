Aurora (formerly BaSTPC) is a third party client of STiBaRC, a service where users can create and share posts with other members of the community. BaSTPC began development on 01 September 2018 and was later released on 20 September 2018. BaSTPC was praised for it's use of Material Design and providing an easier user experience compared to other clients. On 29 December, a new third party client began development to replace BaSTPC with better user experience and design. The Aurora BETA was released on 21 January 2019 and ran until the BETA phase ended that same year on 29 April. Aurora has been praised for it's dark theme by STiBaRC president Aldeenyo and usage of material design features and looks.
To use Aurora, you must log in with your STiBaRC account. If you do not have a STiBaRC account, you can create one here at STiBaRC.

Once your account is created, head over to the login page. You will need to allow Aurora access to your STiBaRC account.
The feed (or the homepage) is where you can view the 20 recent posts from the community of STiBaRC. These posts are shown by their date and time of creation. You can view older posts by scrolling down and pressing the "View Older Posts" button. For each post you can view:
  • The post title
  • The creator of the post
  • The up-votes and down-votes of the post
If there is an important announcement, a blue banner will appear above on the feed and alert you of upcoming changes and announcements.

NOTE: As of update 1.7.1, the feed now has two categories. The main feed allows you to view all recent posts on Aurora while the "Following" feed allows you to view only recent posts from users you follow.
To create a post, go to the top of the screen on the navigation bar and select "New Post". Enter the title of your post and then enter your post content below the title box. Optionally, you can attach one image, video or audio file to your post for others to view. You can learn more on attachments here. Please note that if your post violates the STiBaRC Privacy Policy and STiBaRC Terms of Service, it will be deleted. Please be sure to stay up-to-date on Privacy Policy changes and Terms of Service updates as the STiBaRC Team alerts the community if there are changes made. Please note posts cannot be blank and will not upload if any of the fields are blank!
Editing your post on Aurora is quite simple! Editing posts is a special perk given to users who donated to STiBaRC. Donors get a special donor role on their user page as well. To edit one of your posts, find the post you wish to edit. On the post page, the "edit post" button is next to the voting buttons. When you successfully edit your post, the post will now display a "Post Edited " tag. Please note that when editing posts, your edited post will not update if the title and / or content text areas are blank.
Aurora features a settings page where you can easily change and review settings of your STiBaRC account as well as your settings here on Aurora. Settings can be accessed from Navigation Bar > Profile Photo Dropdown > Settings. The following settings are on Aurora:
  • My Account
  • Security & Privacy
  • Themes
  • Help
  • Legal & Terms
  • Feedback
  • Report Bug
  • About Aurora
To change browser notification settings, go to your browser settings and disable notifications for "https://svaurora.gq/".
Aurora Messenger is a third party client of STiBaRC Messenger. Aurora Messenger works with the main client and other third party clients just as Aurora does with other clients. You can send and receive messages from any user of STiBaRC. Please note that currently, Aurora Messenger does not feature a block user feature. You may report the user from the chat by selecting the settings cogwheel on the navigation bar and scroll down Quick View to "Report User".

You can send and recieve encrypted messages on Aurora Messenger thanks to ASTiW and STiBaRC's encrypted messaging feature. To enable encrypted messaging, be sure to discuss with the recipient of an encryption key password beforehand. You must either create the key or recieve the key from the recipient to use encrpyted messaging. Select the "encrypted messaging" button below of the screen. Enter your encryption password. If the key was correct, your messages should now be disabled. If the messages are still encrypted, disable encrypted messaging and try again.

You can send and recieve files on Aurora Messenger thanks to ASTiW and STiBaRC's file upload feature. Simply select the file upload button and upload any file of your choice. Please note that sometimes, the file may not download correctly. Currently, some files will fail to upload or take a long time. It is recommended to upload files that are small in size and / or compressed.
On select pages, you can view your STiBaRC profile page as well as other user's profile pages. The following information you gave when creating or editing your account is displayed:
  • Your username
  • Your real name
  • Your bio
  • Your email
  • Your birthday
  • Your rank
  • The amount of users that follow you and the amount of users you follow
  • Your posts on STiBaRC
  • The users you follow
  • Your followers
The following information can be changed or hidden on your profile page:
  • Your real name
  • Your birthday
  • Your email
These settings can be changed in Settings > My Account > Edit Profile.

QuickView

QuickView allows users to quickly view a users profile page by loading:
  • Your username
  • Your real name
  • Your bio
  • Your email
  • Your birthday
  • Your rank
QuickView is currently available on Aurora Messenger and will be added in other places in Aurora in the future.
