Reset Aurora

Resetting Aurora will erase the localStorage, which stores your personal Aurora settings and your login status. This action CANNOT BE UNDONE.

By clicking / tapping the "RESET" button, you acknowledge the following will be erased from the localStorage:
  • Your login status
  • Your personal Aurora settings which includes:
    • The set theme
    • Post order on profile pages
    • Alerts
    • Notifcations
  • Aurora's update detection
In addition, the following account will be logged out:
User

Your STiBaRC account and any information stored in the STiBaRC servers will not be affected from the localStorage being erased.

Once the localStorage is erased, you will be returned to the Feed.
